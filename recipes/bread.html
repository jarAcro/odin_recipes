Bread Recipe

Description:

Homemade artisan bread is a delightful and comforting staple in many households. This simple yet satisfying recipe allows you to create a golden-brown, crusty loaf with a soft and tender interior right in your own kitchen. The aroma of freshly baked bread wafting through your home is reason enough to give this recipe a try!

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (about 110째F or 43째C)
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, for yeast activation)
  1. Activate the Yeast:

    In a small bowl, combine the warm water and sugar (if using). Sprinkle the yeast over the water's surface. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy and bubbly. This indicates that the yeast is active and ready to use.

  2. Mix the Dough:

    In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Pour in the activated yeast mixture. Stir until the dough comes together, and then knead it on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

  3. First Rise:

    Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a damp kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

  4. Shape the Loaf:

    After the first rise, gently punch down the dough to release any air bubbles. Shape it into a round or oval loaf.

  5. Second Rise:

    Place the shaped dough on a baking sheet or in a greased loaf pan. Cover it again and let it rise for another 30 minutes to an hour.

  6. Preheat and Score:

    Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C). If desired, make a few shallow slashes (score) on the surface of the dough using a sharp knife or razor blade. This allows the bread to expand and gives it an artisan look.

  7. Bake:

    Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until it's golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.

  8. Cool and Enjoy:

    Allow the bread to cool on a wire rack for at least 30 minutes before slicing. Serve slices warm with butter or your favorite spreads.
